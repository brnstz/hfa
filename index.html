FANvantage

Powered by Foursquare

Welcome! Fanvantage is a sports based web application that's built off of the Foursquare API. The purpose of Fanvantage is simple. It's to see who's fan's will travel to see their beloved teams.

This idea came about during the Foursquare Hackathon in September. Even though we didn't win, we felt we built a pretty awesome application that caputred some pretty important and meaningful data, and it was also well recieved by our peers. The BETA release will be coming out soon, and hopefully we'll be able to fully launch everything in April, before the start of the baseball season.
